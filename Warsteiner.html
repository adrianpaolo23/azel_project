Warsteiner have been brewing beer in Warstein since 1753. As a family-run company with more than 265 years of history, we are proud that we are successful with our Warsteiner Premium Pilsener in over 50 countries.

WARSTEINER BREWERS GOLD
ABV: 5.2%

Warsteiner Brewers Gold is an unfiltered, slightly cloudy amber lager, with notes of caramel and honey in its flavor profile. Its brewing process combines dark Munich and light Pilsner malt, resulting in a well-balanced hoppy beer with a slightly sweet taste.

WARSTEINER PREMIUM FRESH
ABV: 0.0%

It is a light, non-alcoholic beer with a full-bodied, slightly malty taste and a fresh bubbly character. Another reason for its popularity is its low calorie content (23 kcal/100ml). Unlike many other non-alcoholic beers sold on the Estonian market, Warsteiner Premium Fresh really does contain 0% alcohol.

WARSTEINER PREMIUM VERUM
ABV: 4.8%

The aroma is a combination of the light nuances of roasted malt and the grassy character of hops. The taste is dry and crisp. Its fullness is light, the taste is dominated by the creamy, roasted flavor of malt, and the bitter flavors of the hops form a lasting aftertaste.
