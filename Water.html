HIGH-QUALITY FINNISH MINERAL WATERS

AGE  has a wide range of different mineral waters sold under two brands: Amrita, and Montana Alps Natural Alkaline Mineral Water. All AGE mineral waters are made with pure groundwater from the Nordic Lake District, giving them a natural and fresh taste. Mineral waters quench your thirst, complement meals and can be used as the basis for punches and trendy mocktails. You will certainly find your favorite among the wide range of products. MONTANA ALPS

The natural volcanic filtration that Montana Alps undergoes beneath the surface makes it so pure that nothing needs to be added or taken away. MONTANA ALPS is bottled at the source to preserve its elements and unique properties.

AMRITA HIGH PH WATER

Natural alkaline water, such as Amrita natural alkaline mineral water has a chemical makeup that’s vastly different from the artificial one and contains a wealth of minerals.
