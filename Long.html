LONG DRINKS

AGE manufactures long drinks in Finland under the  A. Le Coq, PURE SPIRIT Hard Seltzer and Johan Freitag brands. Johan Freitag is a premium long drink manufactured by fermentation. We recommend that all long drinks be enjoyed chilled on ice!
