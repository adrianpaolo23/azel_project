BEERS

The raw materials for our beers are carefully chosen and mostly of Scandinavian origin. Only the hops are imported because the Scandinavian weather prevents the cultivation of hops of a sufficient quality. Our beers differ from each other by choices of raw materials and production methods. Our beers are matured slowly and patiently, using the one and only correct method. VOLFAS ENGELMAN

“Volfas Engelman” is a beer trademark that has deep historical roots – it’s first registration dates takes back to 1927 after the amalgamation of I.B. Volfas and Engelman breweries. It was revived in the independent Lithuania in 2008. That is the only beer trademark in Lithuania the name of which consists of the last names of two establishers of the brewery.
