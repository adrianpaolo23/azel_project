REFRESHING COCKTAIL – READY TO GO!

Le Coq cocktails are ready to drink and represent the trendiest cocktail flavors of the world. A wide range of classic cocktails is available – get familiar with the fresh drinks and be delighted.

LE COQ ORANGE SPRITZ
ABV: 4.7%

NEW TASTE! Orange Spritz – a well-known taste that reminds you of Aperol Spritz cocktail and the long, lazy and enjoyable hours spent on a lovely vacation day. Orange Spritz has been the favorite cocktail in Europe for past few years now.

LE COQ COCKTAIL MARGARITA
ABV: 4.7%

The flavor of watermelon gives this red-colored southern cocktail a pleasantly juicy taste. Classically, people drink Margaritas on a hot summer’s day, but in our climate it’s also enjoyable during the cold winter months.

LE COQ COCKTAIL MOJITO
ABV: 4.7%

Mojito is a world-famous cocktail which nicely combines the fun tastes of white rum, lime, peppermint and brown sugar. To add special freshness and coolness, add ice to your drink.

LE COQ BLUE LAGOON
ABV: 4.7%

Bright blue alcoholic cocktail with the taste of curacao, pineapple and coconut

T-QUILA SUNRISE
ABV: 4.7%

T-quila Sunrise is an alcoholic cocktail with the taste of tequila, orange juice and Grenadine syrup. If you add a cube of ice to the drink, its color will remind of the sun rays at dawn. Hence the name of the drink.

LE COQ SEX ON THE BEACH 
ABV: 4.7%

A hint to passionate vacations – the taste of juicy orange, peach, cranberry and rum.

LE COQ COSMOPOLITAN
ABV: 4.7%

The flavor of this legendary drink brings to mind red cranberries, sour lime and sugary oranges.

LE COQ PINA COLADA
ABV: 4.7%

Pina Colada cocktail tastes of southern sea rum and coconut milk. Freshness is added by juicy pineapple. This cocktail is the perfect drink for summertime trips as well as cosy indoor get-togethers when it gets colder.

LE COQ CUBA LIBRE 
ABV: 4.7%

This classic cocktail has a taste of rum and cola. It tastes best when served cold with a slice of lemon or lime.
