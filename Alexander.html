SINCE 1807

Alexander brand has three tastes: lager, dunkel and weizen. All these products are full-bodied, strong and differentiating character beers. Alexander is one of the best-selling brand in A. Le Coq portfolio. A. Le coq is the oldest brewery in Estonia dating back to 1807 when it was founded in Prussia. A. Le Coq brewery is still operating in the same place where it was settled by the Le Coq family long time ago.

ALEXANDER WEIZEN
ABV: 5.0%

Alexander Weizen is a double fermented classic German-style wheat beer. Double fermentation means that partly non-fermented beer is added to beer that has already finished fermenting, which results in double fermentation. The beer has a fruity sweet and sour flavor and is rich in citrusy freshness.

ALEXANDER DUNKEL
ABV: 4.2%

Alexander Dunkel is a dark and exceptionally light lager with a beautiful deep brown color. The velvety taste of Dunkel is moderately sweet with hints of aromatic hops.

ALEXANDER UNFILTERED
ABV: 5.0%

The new Alexander is an unfiltered lager with a rich palette, a light sweetness and the rich caramel of barley. The golden amber beer has the aroma of citrus and caramel. We recommend storing the can upside down, turning the can before opening brings out the best qualities of unfiltered beer.

ALEXANDER  Pale lager 
ABV: 5.2%

The full-bodied flavor of Alexander is clearly dominated by the bitter crispness of hops and it is balanced out by the stronger than average caramel flavor of the malt. Fresh hops linger in the finish of this light yellow lager with its dense bubbles.
